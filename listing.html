He must be a very happy pug to be offering such a generous bonus to new players, and you too can share his happiness by playing on a great selection of bingo games, including the classic favourite Deal or No Deal Bingo. Great prizes like exotic holidays, tickets to gigs and iPads are often up for grabs. Vernons Bingo certainly has a unique look to it, what with the interesting combination of bright purple and neon green for a colour scheme! Is anything else quite as electrifying on the site?

Vernons Bingo Games

  • Cash Cubes Bingo – This game uses 36 balls, and instead of numerous tickets, players are given one ticket with 12 numbers across 4 joined cubes. When all the numbers on a square are drawn, it smashes, and a cube drops to the bottom – smash all four and it’s full house! Each cube takes its own slot, and is banked and saved in your Cash Cubes collection. If you manage to collect 50 cubes of one colour, you’ll win a bonus – the value of which depends on the colour you’ve collected. Each game you can select from 4 different colour stake levels, with orange tickets at 10p, green tickets at 50p, purple tickets at £1 and red tickets at £2. So which will you choose? The more you stake, the more you can win! When you pick a colour, your screen will also take on that colour. There’s also a jackpot – it’s progressive, and can be won if you get all 12 numbers across 4 cubes in 18 calls or less. The winner will get half of the jackpot total and the remaining 50% will be split between all the other players.
  • Who Wants to be a Millionaire is a fun 90 ball bingo game based on a much-loved TV show. There are 12 jackpots to be played for. For example, just call House within 25 or 26 balls to win the £75,000 Jackpot. Entry to the chat rooms is right there too. Check out the Schedule for ticket prices, minimum prizes and game start times.
  • Rainbow Riches Bingo uses tickets in the shape of four leaf clover, just to get you in the mood for this fun, fast and furious Irish themed game which features a leprechaun moving along a rainbow,  a Wishing Well feature, a magic number and if you’re lucky a cache of golden coins. In each game there are four prizes up for grabs: 1 Clover, House, Rainbow Riches Jackpot Prize, and the Wishing Well prize. While there is a minimum guaranteed prize on offer, the final payout will depend on the number of tickets purchased. In this game two or more players can share a prize. Check out the Schedule for ticket prices, minimum prizes and game start times. You can join in the chat too, and tap the Help/Bingo/Rainbow Riches tab for all the details. Terms and Conditions apply.

18+. New players only. Wagering requirements apply. See website for full T&Cs.
